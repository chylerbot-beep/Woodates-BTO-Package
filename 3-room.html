FAQ

Package details and frequently asked questions

Does the package include everything, or are there add-ons I'll have to pay for? ▾

The package includes everything you need for a fully renovated home with plenty of storage and easy-to-clean surfaces:

  • 3D Design (up to 2 revisions)
  • Personal Project Manager
  • All carpentry uses premium ultra low-formaldehyde E0 plywood.
    20ft Top and Bottom Kitchen Cabinets.
    1x Full Height 1800mm Common Bedroom Wardrobe.
  • 10ft Sintered Stone Countertop
  • Choose between Tiles or LVT Vinyl Flooring (with flooring package)
  • Cement Screeding
  • 2x Tempered Glass Fixed Shower Panel for both bathrooms
  • Painting for the entire house (Nippon, up to 3 colours)
  • Includes standard Plumbing and Electrical Works

Customizations like feature walls, false ceilings, kitchen partitions, or additional carpentry are quoted separately and agreed before we start.

Can I visit a completed project before I sign? ▾
Yes — and we actively encourage it. Seeing a finished home in person is the best way to understand the quality of our carpentry, how accurate our renders are, and what your home will actually feel like to live in. WhatsApp us and we'll arrange a site visit to a recently completed project that matches your preferred style. Clients who visit a site before signing consistently feel most confident about their decision.
When should I contact you relative to my key collection date? ▾
As early as possible. A standard 3-room BTO renovation takes 6–10 weeks. We recommend reaching out as soon as you have a confirmed key collection date — or even before. The earlier we begin design, the better your chances of securing a renovation slot that aligns with your move-in target.
How does payment work — do I need to pay a lot upfront? ▾
Payment is split into 5 milestones tied to completed work stages: 15% at signing, 35% upon commencement of work, 20% upon completion of tiling or flooring works, 25% upon delivery of carpentry work, 5% upon completion of work. You never pay for the next stage until the current one is verified.
Do you do 4-room, 5-room, resale, condo or landed as well? ▾
Yes! Our Signature Package is also available for 4-room BTOs. We take on 5-room BTO, resale HDB, condo, and landed projects too — these are quoted individually based on scope. WhatsApp us with your property type, floor plan and key collection date and we'll work on the quote for you.
